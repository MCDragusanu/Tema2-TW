Project Workout Companion

Workout Companion is a user-friendly fitness app designed to enhance your workout experience. Tailored to individual needs, it generates custom workouts that align with your fitness goals and preferences. What sets this app apart is its focus on intelligent tracking and the automation of progressive overload. By consistently monitoring your progress, Workout Companion ensures that your workouts evolve over time, providing a personalized and effective fitness journey. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or a beginner, this app acts as a reliable companion, making your workouts more engaging and results-driven.

Codelingo

CodeLingo is an innovative app designed to assist software developers in refining their interview skills. With a focus on practical preparation, the app integrates an AI assistant, providing a realistic simulation of interview scenarios. Users can practice a variety of interview types, from technical assessments to behavioral questions, while receiving personalized feedback from the AI. CodeLingo aims to create a dynamic and effective environment for developers to enhance their interview readiness, making it a valuable tool for both beginners and experienced professionals in the software development field.
